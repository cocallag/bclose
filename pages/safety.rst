Fire safety
-----------

•	In the event of a fire raise the alarm and evacuate if it is safe to do so.
•	There is a fire blanket in the kitchen on the right-hand side of the door as you enter the kitchen. 
•	There is a fire extinguisher just outside the kitchen in the hall.
