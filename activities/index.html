    Things to do

    Oxford is a great place to live and the apartmet is near to the city centre as well as Port Meadow. There are shops nearby on Walton Street or North Parade.

    The University Parks are a short walk away and are a great place for a stroll or a run. You can also walk to Port Meadow from the apartment which is a lovely open space by the river. There are many museums and galleries in Oxford including the Ashmolean Museum, the Museum of Natural History, and the Pitt Rivers Museum. You can also visit the Bodleian Library, which is one of the oldest libraries in Europe.

    There are many restaurants, cafes, and pubs in Oxford. Some popular areas for dining out include Jericho and the city centre. For shopping, you can visit the Westgate Shopping Centre in the city centre or explore the independent shops on Walton Street and in Jericho. There are also many events and festivals that take place in Oxford throughout the year, such as the Oxford Literary Festival and the Oxford Food Festival. In summer consider a punt on the river or a boat trip from Folly Bridge.
